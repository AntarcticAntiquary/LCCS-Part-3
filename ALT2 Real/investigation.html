Deciding on the topic

We had many options of datasets to consider for our project

Wespent most of the first day researching various types of databases

Our main contenders were CSO census data, kaggle, and WHO Covid data

We quickly settled on something to do with Covid, however - as there was so much relevant data


Hypothesis

We considered measuring if Covid was more prevalent in urban or rural areas

However, our other option was much easier - measuring how well different countries reacted to Covid

We decided to measure this by the countries' ratio of deaths to cases

There were several good datasets for this, but we chose one on kaggle
