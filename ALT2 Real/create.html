Problems Encountered

We encountered a determined error that took us a long time to fix

The graph would plot the y-axis values all out of order

After extensive troubleshooting, we realised that the dataset had those numbers in a format with commas (e.g. 12,374 instead of 12374)

This meant that when read into python, they became strings instead of integers

We replaced all the commas with nothing and used loops to turn every data piece into floats

And that solved the problem!
